About Us

Xenesis 2018

Xenesis basically stands for the term evolution. X in Xenesis stands for extreme. Xenesis is one of the most popular tech fest among techno geeks of Gujarat, with average 1500 participants and more than 2000 visitors since the inception in 2007. Xenesis 2018 will be held on 15th and 16th of February. In this season of Xenesis we have included all new events, some amazing workshops/seminars and X-potentially guaranteed fun.
